Help!


  • Double-click anywere on the screen to get a fresh new sticklet.
  • Visit the archive menu to permanently delete or restore a sticklet.
  • Offline? You can move, color, and read your sticklets in the browser! Any changes are rolled back when next online.
  • Hit tab or escape to quickly close an open text area and save. Otherwise, click off of the area to be sure it saves.
  • Control-z to undo quickly.
  • Control-y to redo quickly.
  • Shift-? (question mark) to see this help menu.
